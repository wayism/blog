Between Words

Between Words

A meditation on finding temples in ordinary moments

Here
               where lizards pray
                              in sun-warmed silence

a tortilla’s steam rises
               like morning incense
                              salted with grace

market
               voices weave
                              through air thick with
                                             ripening mangos and memory

somewhere
               a young heart
                              steals its first kiss
                                             while street dogs
                    map their faith
                        in dusty paws

pebbles
               cup centuries
                              in rough palms

ordinary miracles
               scatter like
            feeding birds
                   between streetlight
                           and shadow
